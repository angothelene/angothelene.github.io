### About me

I am a CNRS Research Scientist, at the [Institute of Environmental Geosciences](https://www.ige-grenoble.fr/) in Grenoble (France). My research focuses on the impact of global changes on the cycle of contaminants (e.g., mercury, microplastics). I currently lead research projects in remote regions of the world, including the Arctic, the French Alps, and the Southern Ocean, and use both field observations and modeling tools.
